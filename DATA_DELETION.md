# Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Users can request deletion of data related to Charbel Business AI Agent by emailing:

charbellebbousf@gmail.com

In the request, include:

- The WhatsApp phone number used to contact the business
- A short statement requesting deletion of your conversation data

After receiving the request, the project owner will identify matching conversation records, message records, and audit records and delete them where legally and operationally appropriate.

This project uses the official Meta WhatsApp Business Platform / Cloud API. Some records may also be retained by Meta according to Meta's own platform policies and retention rules.
