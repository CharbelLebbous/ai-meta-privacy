# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

## Service

Charbel Business AI Agent is a WhatsApp Business Platform automation project used to receive and respond to WhatsApp messages from explicitly allowed contacts.

## Data We Collect

When a user contacts the WhatsApp business number, the service may process and store:

- WhatsApp phone number identifiers
- Message content sent to the business number
- Message timestamps and delivery status events
- Conversation state, audit logs, and handoff status
- Media/document metadata when supported files are sent

## How We Use Data

We use this data to:

- Receive inbound WhatsApp messages
- Generate and send allowed automated replies
- Enforce contact allowlisting and security controls
- Keep audit logs for abuse prevention, troubleshooting, and operational review
- Support manual handoff when AI auto-reply is disabled

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Data may be processed by service providers needed to operate the system, including:

- Meta WhatsApp Business Platform / Cloud API
- The configured AI provider, if live AI mode is enabled
- Hosting, database, and logging infrastructure used by the project owner

## Data Retention

Conversation records and audit logs are retained only as long as needed for operation, debugging, security review, and compliance with business requirements. The project owner may delete records earlier upon request.

## Security

The service uses server-side secret handling, webhook verification, contact allowlisting, audit logging, and restricted AI auto-reply controls. No WhatsApp Web scraping, browser automation, or unofficial WhatsApp APIs are used.
